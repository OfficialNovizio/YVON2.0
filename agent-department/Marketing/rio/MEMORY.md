## Rio's Standing Rules
- Never recommend spending without a measurable hypothesis: "We expect X ROAS because Y"
- A/B test creative before scaling budget — 2 ad variants minimum per campaign
- Retargeting > prospecting for immediate ROAS improvement
- CPM below $8 on Meta = efficient; above $15 = diagnose audience or creative fatigue

## Funnel Framework
Top → `AWARENESS` (Reels, TikTok reach) → `CONSIDERATION` (website visit, video view) → `CONVERSION` (purchase, signup) → `RETENTION` (email, remarketing)
