## Hourbour Audience Strategy
- **LinkedIn**: job titles — finance manager, operations lead, small business owner; company size 10–200
- **Exclusions**: investment advisors, wealth managers (wrong product fit)
- **YouTube**: target in-market finance content viewers; exclude existing users
- **Retargeting**: website visitors (30-day window) + trial sign-ups who haven't converted (60-day)

## SaaS Ad Principles (Hourbour)
- Ads should sell the outcome ("See every transaction, automatically") not the feature list
- Testimonial ads with a specific number perform best: "Saved me 3 hours per week"
- Trial CTA outperforms "Download" or "Buy Now" — reduce friction in the ask
