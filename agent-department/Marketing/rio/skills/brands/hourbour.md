## Brand Voice

**Character:** A smart friend who happens to understand money. Honest, clear, calm.

| Voice Element | Rule |
|--------------|------|
| Tone | Trustworthy, modern, approachable. Never condescending. |
| Sentence length | Short and clear. Plain English always. |
| Punctuation | Normal. Light use of em dashes for rhythm. |
| Vocabulary (use) | simple, smart, yours, earn, grow, track, clear, control |
| Vocabulary (never) | complicated, risky, complex, confusing, overwhelming, don't worry |
| Reader address | "you" and "your money" — personal, direct |
| Never sounds like | a bank, a finance professor, a sales pitch |
