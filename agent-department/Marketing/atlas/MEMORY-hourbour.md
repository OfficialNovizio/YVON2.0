## Venture Context
- **Aesthetic**: clean, modern, tech-forward — clarity and trust, not clinical
- **Positioning**: financial clarity for people who want control, not complexity

## Hourbour Visual System
| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Lighting | Bright, even — conveys clarity and openness |
| Background | White or light grey preferred; data-viz on dark only if brand-spec'd |
| UI/Product | App screenshots preferred — show real functionality, not abstract mockups |
| People | Professionals in natural work environments — not stock "business handshake" |
| Data viz | Graphs/charts welcome — make numbers beautiful and legible |
| Colour palette | Brand primary colours + white space; avoid colour overload |

## What to Always Avoid (Hourbour)
- Stock imagery of coins, piggy banks, cash stacks, or gold bars — clichéd fintech visuals
- Dark moody backgrounds (unless specific brand-approved campaign)
- Overly abstract or illustrative styles that obscure the product
- Complicated layouts that feel as dense as a bank statement

## Prompt Architecture Notes (Hourbour)
- Lead prompt with: setting description → UI element → lighting → mood adjectives
- Use: "clean studio light", "minimal white background", "app UI on device"
- For people shots: "candid professional at desk", "natural light office" — not "businessman"
- Test: "Stripe-era fintech aesthetic" for instant Hourbour register
