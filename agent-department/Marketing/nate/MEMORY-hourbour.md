## Hourbour Growth Levers
1. **Activation optimization** — getting users to their first transaction sync is the #1 lever; everything before paid conversion depends on this
2. **K-factor / referral** — "Invite a colleague, both get 1 month free"; professional SaaS has high referral potential
3. **LinkedIn organic** — thought leadership content from the founder increases trial sign-ups; target finance professionals
4. **Trial email sequence** — 5-email onboarding: day 0 setup, day 1 first win, day 3 tip, day 7 check-in, day 14 upgrade nudge
