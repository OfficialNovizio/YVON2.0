## Hourbour Voice Rules
- **Never say**: complicated, risky, complex, volatile, bet, gamble, wealth
- **Never use**: finance-bro language, excessive acronyms, fear-based messaging
- **Do say**: clear, simple, trackable, reliable, automatic, in control
- **Sentence style**: short, active voice — "See exactly where your money goes." not "Our platform provides comprehensive financial visibility."
- **Emoji**: 1 max per post; prefer functional (📊, ✅) over decorative
