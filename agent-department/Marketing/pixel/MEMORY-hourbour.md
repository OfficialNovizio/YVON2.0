## Hourbour QC Criteria
Standard 3-pass QC for every Hourbour batch:
1. **Clarity check** — is the image clean and uncluttered? Does it communicate trust?
2. **Brand fit check** — does it look like a modern fintech product, not a bank or stock photo?
3. **No-cliché check** — zero coins, piggy banks, cash stacks, or "businessman handshake" imagery?
