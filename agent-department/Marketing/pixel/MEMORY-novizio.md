## Novizio QC Criteria
Standard 3-pass QC for every Novizio batch:
1. **Lighting check** — is it natural, soft, directional? No harsh shadows or over-lit studio?
2. **Background check** — is it clean and neutral? No busy patterns or promotional clutter?
3. **Energy check** — does the image feel editorial (magazine spread) or commercial (catalogue)?
